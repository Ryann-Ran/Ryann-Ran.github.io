I am Yuran Wang, a first-year master's student in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Peking University.

From 2022 to 2025, I worked on computer vision under the supervision of [Prof. Zheng Wang](https://wangzwhu.github.io/home/) at AIM Lab, Wuhan University.

Since 2024, I have been with DCAI Lab at Peking University under the supervision of [Prof. Wentao Zhang](https://zwt233.github.io/).

My research interests include *visual generation* and *multimodal understanding*. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in collaborating.
